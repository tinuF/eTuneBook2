Welcome to eTuneBook


With eTuneBook you can

  • Manage Abc tunes in your browser.
  • Edit tunes by editing Abc text (Tune-Editor).
  • Make sets out of tunes.
  • Make playlists out sets.
  • Add Repeat Time and diverse Annotations how a tune should be played whithin a playlist
  • View the first bars of a tune.
  • Remember when you last played a tune.
  • Label tunes with a color.
  • Transpose tunes.
  • Store links to other sites holding tune informations.
  • Store links to YouTube videos and view them in eTuneBook directly.
  • Search tunes and sets by title.
  • Filter tunes and sets by type, key, color, event, band.
  • Sort tunes and sets by id, play date, frequency. Shuffle tunes and sets.
  • Import Abc tunes from a File on your hard disk or from your Google Drive.
  • Export Abc tunes to a File on your hard disk or to your Google Drive.

Import/Export via local hard disk does not work on mobile iOS devices. You have to use Google Drive instead.

eTuneBook is free of use. If you find it useful, please please please donate for nature conservation.
