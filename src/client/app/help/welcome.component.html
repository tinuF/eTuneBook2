Welcome to eTuneBook


With eTuneBook you can

  • Manage your TuneBook in the Browser
  • Edit Abc tunes
  • Combine tunes to sets
  • Combine sets to playlists
  • Remember when you last played a tune
  • Label tunes with a color
  • Transpose tunes
  • Store web links for tunes
  • Store links to YouTube videos and view them in eTuneBook directly. Side by Side with the dots.
  • Search tunes, sets and playlists by tune title
  • Filter tunes and sets by tune type, key, or color, or by playlist criteria
  • Sort tunes and sets by tune name, id, play date, frequency. Shuffle tunes and sets
  • Tune randomizer following filter settings and title search
  • Import Abc tunes from a File
  • Export Abc tunes to a File
    • (does not work on iPad, iPhone)

eTuneBook works offline in the Browser

  • You don't need Internet connection once eTuneBook is loaded
  • Your TuneBook is kept in the local storage of the browser, not on the server
  • Since your Tunebook is only kept locally, make sure to export your book as abc file at the end of the day!
  • On the next day, before you start rehearsing and editing with your TuneBook, you should re-import your Tunebook. This helps to tell, whether all is ok with your abc file.

eTuneBook is free of use

  • If you find it useful, please donate for nature conservation.
  • Use it on your own risk. Best practice to minimize risks: Backup your TuneBook at the end of the day!
